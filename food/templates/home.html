About Us

The movement aims to reduce food waste and to increase appreciation of food products. This involves next to sharing food often also educational activities. The initiative Foodsharing for example also draws attention to food waste and the issue of unnecessary packaging via events, activities and media relations. It is not needed anymore to dispose of unwanted and overproduced food products, but these products are shared with consumers via distributors and online communities which makes it innovative.
