Some instructions for Sharing food

Donated food should be handled safely and according to state or local food codes.

Make sure food packages are properly labeled.
For example, foods that contain major food allergens must list the allergens on the product label.

Allergens Foods that have not been opened should be donated in their original packaging.

Store foods safely. Keep away from sources of contamination,
such as dust or debris, spoiled or recalled foods, and from other products like cleaning supplies.

Keep foods at their safe temperatures. Hot foods should be stored and held at 135°F or above, and
cold foods should be stored and held at 41°F or below.
